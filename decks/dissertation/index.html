Key principles

  • Basis and signal are correlated quantities
    • Knowledge of basis does not reveal knowledge of signal
  • Selection of key is always unique
    • \(N\) bits sent, \(n\) bits used (\(n < N\))
  • Statistics of measurement can reveal information about transaction
