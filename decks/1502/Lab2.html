Lab 2: Reflection and Refraction

Reflection
the throwing back of light [or heat or sound] without absorbing it
Refraction
the bending of light as it is transmitted through a material
Reflection
$$\theta_i = \theta_r$$
Refraction
$$\frac{\sin\theta_i}{\sin\theta_t} = \frac{v_i}{v_t} = \frac{\lambda_i}{\lambda_t}$$
