Lab 1: Measurement and Uncertainty

Primer: How do we know the mass of an electron?
Simpler: How do we know what 1000 kg is?
Why should we care?
  • It allows us to determine if the difference between two independent results is real, or just due to sloppy measurement
Where does uncertainty come from?
  • The measuring instrument
  • The item being measured
  • The measurement process
  • "Imported" uncertainties
  • Operator skill
  • Sampling issues
  • The environment
Tips for reducing uncertainty
  • Calibrate your instrument
  • Make your measurements traceable to national standards
  • Choose the best measurement instrument
  • Repeat measurements
  • Use rounding correctly in your calculations
