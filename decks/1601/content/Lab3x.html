Method 1
Avg time $=\bar{t} \rightarrow \bar{\Delta t} = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{N}}$
Then use $r = \frac{V}{t}$ and propagate uncertainty

Method 2
Compute $r = \frac{V}{t}$ from each $t$ measurement
Then take $\mathrm{(avg\;rate)} = \bar{r} \rightarrow \bar{\Delta r} = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{N}}$

Other valid methods exist, but they are less robust
