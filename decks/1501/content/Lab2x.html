Clarification on policies

When to use absolute or relative uncertainty

Absolute
Adding and subtracting
Has units:
$(63.1 \pm 0.2)~\mathrm{cm} \;\;$ or $\;\; 63.1~\mathrm{cm} \pm 0.2~\mathrm{cm}$
Relative
Multiplying or dividing
No units:
$63.1~\mathrm{cm} \pm 0.2 \;\;$ or $\;\; 63.1~\mathrm{cm} \pm 20\%$
